Welcome to African Audio Works

African Audio Works was established in 2012 by its founder and owner, Andile Sasanti. He is a professional producer, sound engineer and DJ whom has been actively involved in the entertainment industry for more than 7 years.

He worked as a DJ in most venues securing residency and playing as a guest early years in the industry. This assisted in gaining more experience working with the latest hi-tech equipment and getting more exposure into the entertainment industry. His experience in the industry includes setting up and operating of sound for various promotions, conferences, corporate events, government rallies, individuals and night clubs. He is currently a music producer for a number of South African artists.

African Audio Works also started a record and publishing company during the course of 2015. Since then our very own artists are leading charts on some of the South African radio stations.

African Audio Works provides complete event solutions to various sectors of the entertainment industry. Not only do we supply the equipment itself, but also professional and experienced DJs to ensure that your event will be nothing less than a roaring success. Our field of expertise covers various types of events, from small conferences to large scale corporate functions.

African Audio Works is proud supplier of WHARFEDALE to ensure quality and that there will be no shortfall to supply our clients on demand- whether the event is booked weeks in advance or a day before the time. We supply professional equipment for the following:

Promotion/Gatherings
Corporate Events
Fashion Shows
Music Concerts
Sports Events
Shows/Plays
Banquets & Gala Dinners
Year End functions
Birthdays/Weddings and Unveiling services

We have a wide range of professional speaker systems, mixing consoles and microphones (wired and wireless systems) to give you exactly the sound you want: clear, powerful, sensational sound.

A selection of professional DJ equipment (mixers, CDJs, headphones & more) is also available For your visual requirements we have a number of data projectors with crystal clear imaging, bringing new dimension to any presentation or video. Play back equipment such as DVD players and laptops are also available on request.
